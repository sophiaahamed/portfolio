Science has given us the ability to understand our own minds and bodies. Art has given us the ability to communicate these findings with others. The goal is to create a different kind of process, one that stems from the artist and to the viewer. As a collective its important to provide our clients & audience with functional creative outlets that will help shape the future of art, science and technology.

stuidoahamed is a design collective with a focus on building connections within creative communities through the means of simplicity & complexity. The creative arts is as much of a scientific process as it is a process of free flowing abstraction. The idea is to take a non-traditional look into the creative process and therefore create a new experience between the artist and the viewer, the client and the creative agency.
